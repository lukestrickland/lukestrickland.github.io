--- layout: post title: "FIPS release" description: "Code" mathjax: true --- Michael David Wilson, Timothy Ballard and I have recently released the Fatigue Impairment Prediction Suite, an open source R package implementing biomathematical models of fatigue. In addition to the front-facing package content, we are actively developing and testing Bayesian parameter estimation of fatigue models using Stan.

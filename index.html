I'm Luke. I'm a Post-Doctoral Fellow at the Future of Work Institute, Curtin University.

I'm a scientist and computational modeller. In my current role, I apply biomathematical models of human fatigue to inform rostering and workforce decisions. My research spans cognitive science, human factors, and quantitative methods. For example, I have developed computational models to help understand the psychological processes underlying conflict detection in air traffic control.

I am an avid user of R and Python, particularly R. I contribute to R software projects designed to facilitate biomathematical modelling and cognitive modelling.
